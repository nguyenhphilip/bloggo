10 Jan 2018

Winter

Arbiter of cessation, master of endings,
a gust inevitably endured.
Clench your jaw now, the exchange is complete:
Snow and frost for people and sidewalks.
Inches and burdens
for sauntered footfalls.

There is a season between seasons
a bridge that is crossed by the plentiful few.
Adults and scientists seek it endlessly
though only the children know
of its whereabouts.
But don’t bother with asking––
they’re sworn to the promises of secrecy.

I make no guarantees of my own:
Certainty is certainly
out of the picture.
Though some day you may catch hint of
what it is and
where it may be found.

A clue:
It’s right in front of you.
Between one breath of Winter and
One sigh of Spring
There exists everything
essential.
